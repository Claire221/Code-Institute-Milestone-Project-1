Hogwarts School Staff and Teachers

Headmaster

Proffessor Dumbledore

Albus Dumbledore is the current headmaser at Hogwarts School. He himself was a student at Hogwarts and then returned as a Teacher. He has held many positions within the school including being a Defence Against the Dark Arts Professor, Transfiguration Professor and now Headmaster.

Deputy Headmaster

Professor McGonagall

Professor McGonagall is Hogwarts Schools current deputy head teacher. McGonagall was a student at Hogwarts herself and in her seventh year was appointed head girl. After she graduated Hogwarts, she took up a position within the Ministry of Magic before returning to Hogwarts where she accepted the position of Transfiguration Professor and head of Gryffindor house and finally being appointed the positon of Deputy Head.

Teachers

Potions Teacher

Proffessor Snape

Professor Snape is Hogwarts Schools Potion Teacher. Professor Snape attended Hogwarts in the 1970s and was sorted into Slytherin house. Throughout his time at Hogwarts, he showed a talent for all things potion making. After graduation, Professor Snape accepted a role at Hogwarts School as the Schools Potions Professor and also head of Slytherin house.

Flying Instructor

Madame Hooch

Madam Hooch is Hogwarts Flying Instructor. Madam Hooch is a former Hogwarts student and after graduating she was selected by the Department of Magical Games and Sports and given the position of Flying instructor and Quidditch referee within the school.

Charms Teacher

Professor Flitwick

Professor Flitwick is Hogwarts current Charms Professor. Professor Flitwick is a former Hogwarts School professor who was a member of Ravenclaw House. After Graduation he returned to Hogwarts as our current Charms Master and Head of Ravenclaw House. He is also Hogwarts current Choirmaster, and they put on regular performances throughout the school.

Divination Teacher

Proffessor Trelawney

Professor Trelawney is Hogwarts current Divination Professor and is the great-great granddaughter of the celebrated seer Cassandra Trelawney. Prefessor Trelawney is a former Hogwarts school student who was sorted into Ravenclaw, because of her ancestry she picked up Divination while at school and achieved high grades. After graduation she returned to the school and took the position of Divination Professor.

Defence Against the Dark Arts Teacher

Gilderoy Lockhart

Professor Lockhart is Hogwarts current Defense Against the Dark Arts Teacher. Professor Lockhart is a former Hogwarts student who was a member of Ravenclaw House. After graduating Hogwarts Professor Lockhart went on to be a bestselling Author, He would travel the world and document the adventures he went on and the people and creatures he met. Based on his accomplishments Professor Lockhart was offered the position of Defense Against the Dark arts teacher which he gratefully accepted.

Herbology Teacher

Professor Sprout

Professor Sprout is Hogwarts current Herbology Teacher. Professor Sprout is a former Hogwarts student and member of Hufflepuff house. While at school she excelled in Herbology and not long after graduation returned to Hogwarts where she accepted the position of Herbology professor and Head of Hufflepuff house.

Other Staff

Gamekeeper

Hagrid

Hagrid is Hogwarts Gamekeeper. Hagrid is a former Hogwarts Student who was a member of Gryffindor House. After Graduation he returned to accept the position of Gamekeeper where he maintains Hogwarts school grounds and is in charge of Security. Not only does Hagrid undertake his Gamekeeper duties but he also Is the first point of contact for all first year students and escorts them to the castle on their first day.

Caretaker

Filtch

Filtch is Hogwarts Schools Caretaker. Filtch has been in the position of Caretaker within the school since 1973 and can often be seen wondering the halls with his cat Mrs Norris. He is responsible for maintaining the School building and outhouses and also ensuring students dont go wandering in places they shouldnt be.

School Nurse

Madame Pomfrey

Madame Pomfrey is Hogwarts current Matron and has held the position for the last quarter century. She is very proficient in her area of expertise and lives by her motto of being able to fix any ailment in a heartbeat.
